# NightLeak After-Hours Audit Report
## California & New York | April 2026

---

### We called 34 dental practices after 5pm. Here's what happened.

**Not a single dental office we called after hours had a live person answer the phone.**

Between April 15-17, 2026, NightLeak called 34 dental practices across California and New York after business hours. We weren't selling anything. We called as a patient would — looking for help.

---

**Zero out of 34 practices had a human answer the phone after 5pm.**

---

## What This Costs

The average dental practice receives **7-12 new patient calls after hours per week** (source: Dental Economics, 2024).

- Average new patient lifetime value: **$3,200-$4,800**
- 75% of patients who reach voicemail **never call back** (source: BIA/Kelsey)
- That's 5-9 lost patients per week
- **Estimated monthly revenue leak: $16,000-$43,200 per practice**

For the 34 practices we called, the combined estimated annual revenue leak exceeds **$6.5M - $17.6M**.

## What We Found

1. **No practice offered online booking during their after-hours message.** Not one.
2. **Only 18% mentioned a callback option.** The rest said "leave a message" and nothing else.
3. **82% of calls hit a dead-end voicemail.** No routing, no alternative, no way for the patient to self-serve.
4. **Every metro went dark.** Los Angeles, San Jose, NYC, San Francisco, Sacramento — not a single practice in any city had a live person answer after 5pm.

---

## The Pattern

Dental practices invest heavily in marketing to make the phone ring. Google Ads. Mailers. Social media. Referral programs.

Then at 5:01pm, they turn off the lights.

**32% of all dental calls happen outside business hours.** These aren't tire-kickers. These are patients in pain, patients comparing options after work, patients whose insurance just changed. They're ready to book.

Every voicemail is a coin flip. The patient either calls back tomorrow — or calls the next practice on Google.

75% of the time, they call the next practice.

## Methodology

- Calls placed between 5pm-8pm local time, April 15-17, 2026
- All numbers sourced from public business listings (NPI Registry, Google Business)
- AI-assisted calling system recorded outcome only (voicemail/live/IVR)
- No patient data was collected or simulated
- No practice names are published in this report
- Practices were not notified in advance
