# 60-Second Video Script: "We Called 34 Dental Offices After 5pm"
## DIS YouTube / TikTok / Reels

---

### FORMAT
- Screen recording of color-coded map filling in with dots
- Eric voiceover (or text overlay if no face cam)
- Dark background, NightLeak aesthetic

---

### SCRIPT (60 seconds)

**[0-3s] HOOK — text on screen, say it:**
"We called 34 dental offices after 5pm. One person answered."

**[3-8s] THE SETUP:**
"Every dental practice spends thousands on marketing to make the phone ring. Google Ads. Mailers. Referrals. Then at 5:01, they turn off the lights."

**[8-18s] THE DATA — map starts filling in with red dots:**
"We called 34 practices across California and New York after hours. 82% went straight to voicemail. 15% hit a phone tree. Zero offered online booking. Zero."

**[18-25s] THE COST:**
"32% of dental calls happen after hours. 75% of patients who hit voicemail never call back. For these 34 practices alone, that's an estimated 6 to 17 million dollars in lost revenue per year."

**[25-35s] THE PATTERN — zoom into San Jose cluster:**
"San Jose. Four practices called. Three voicemails. New York. Five practices. Four voicemails. Sacramento. Voicemail. San Francisco. Voicemail."

**[35-45s] THE ONE:**
"One practice in LA had a person answer. One out of 34. That practice is capturing every patient the other 33 are losing."

**[45-55s] THE TURN:**
"This isn't a technology problem. This is a 'nobody told you' problem. Your phone rings after 5. Nobody's there. And the patient calls someone else."

**[55-60s] CTA — nightleakaudit.com on screen:**
"We're auditing every city in California. Want to know your number? Link in bio."

---

### PRODUCTION NOTES

- Map animation: can be screen-recorded from a simple HTML page with dots appearing one by one
- Red dots for voicemail, green for answered — the visual is almost entirely red
- No practice names shown at any point
- Music: low, ambient, slightly tense (royalty-free)
- Thumbnail: map covered in red dots, text "97% DIDN'T ANSWER"
